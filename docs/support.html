Support

Questions, bug reports, and feature requests — all welcome.

Does LastPlace back up or sync my data?

Not yet — everything you save is stored locally on your device only. If you delete the app or lose your device, that data is gone unless you've backed up the device itself another way. Account-based sync is planned for a future update.

Can I delete everything I've saved?

Yes — go to Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data. This permanently removes every room, item, checklist, and photo. This can't be undone.

Does LastPlace work without an internet connection?

Yes. LastPlace works entirely offline — everything is stored locally, so there's no connection required to add, edit, or browse your rooms and items.
